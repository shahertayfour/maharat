About Maharat Training

Founded in 2023 and officially registered under Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) in 2025, Maharat Training serves as a vital bridge connecting talent with evolving industry demands across the UAE and broader region.

We are committed to empowering Emirati and regional professionals through comprehensive workshops, intensive bootcamps, and corporate upskilling programs that align with national strategic initiatives, including the UAE AI Strategy 2031.
